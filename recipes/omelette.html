Omelette

This ham and cheese omelette is so easy and so good.

Nutrition facts(Amount per serving)

  • Calories: 550
  • Fat: 39,5
  • Carbohydrate: 5,5
  • Protein: 40,4

Ingredients

  • Olive oil: 10 grams
  • Ham: 4 slices
  • Eggs: 2 pieces
  • Salt: 1/4 tsp
  • Black pepper: to taste
  • Cheese: 32 grams

Recipe instructions

  1. Heat oil in a skillet over medium heat. Add ham and cook until browned and crispy, stirring frequently, about 7 minutes.
  2. Beat eggs with salt, and pepper in a small bowl.
  3. Pour egg mixture into the skillet; let cook for 30 seconds. Lift the edges of the omelet so that uncooked egg runs under
    cooked edges and comes into contact with the hot skillet. Shake and tilt the skillet to move uncooked egg. Sprinkle cheese on top and fold omelette in half.
    Flip and cook until egg is entirely set and cheese has melted, 30 seconds to 1 minute.
  4. Slide onto a plate.

Omelette is ready
