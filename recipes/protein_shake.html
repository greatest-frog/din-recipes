Protein Shake

A tasty protein shake is the perfect meal for breakfast or a snack.

Nutrition facts(Amount per serving)

  • Calories: 360
  • Fat: 10,4
  • Carbohydrate: 37,2
  • Protein: 31,9

Ingredients

  • Banana(or other fruit, berry): 100g
  • Protein powder 85%: 25g
  • Milk: 300ml

Recipe instructions

  1. Grind a banana in a blender
  2. Pour milk into a shaker
  3. Add protein powder and banana puree
  4. Mix well

Protein shake is ready
